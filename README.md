Sales Performance Dashboard & Business Insights Analysis

Project Overview
This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance and generate business insights from retail sales data. The dashboard helps identify sales trends, profitable categories, high-performing regions, and customer segment contributions.

Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel

Key KPIs
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Profit Margin %

Dashboard Features
- Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
- Sales by Region
- Profit by Category
- Top Products by Sales
- Sales by Segment
- Interactive KPI Monitoring

Business Insights
- West region generated the highest sales revenue.
- Technology category delivered the highest profit.
- Consumer segment contributed the largest share of sales.
- Sales showed strong growth during year-end periods.

Skills Demonstrated
- Data Visualization
- Business Intelligence
- Dashboard Development
- KPI Reporting
- DAX Calculations
- Data Analysis

Project Outcome
Developed an executive-level sales dashboard that transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights for decision-making.
